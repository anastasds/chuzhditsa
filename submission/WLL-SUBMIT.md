# Submitting to Written Language & Literacy — checklist

Everything is prepared; the clicks are yours.

## Files (all in this repo)

| File | Role |
|---|---|
| `paper/chuzhditsa_wll.pdf` | **Anonymized manuscript** (23 pp, 12 pt, 1.5-spaced, fonts embedded; no author, no repo URL) |
| `paper/wll_titlepage.pdf` | Separate title page (author, contact, running head, word count, materials note) |
| `submission/wll_cover_letter.txt` | Cover letter — paste into the submission form or attach |

## Steps

1. Go to https://benjamins.com/catalog/wll and follow its **online submission** link
   (the target is Editorial Manager, https://www.editorialmanager.com/wll/ — if the EM
   site shows an "under development" notice or won't take the submission, the journal's
   documented fallback is the editors' email: **writtenlanglit@gmail.com**).
2. Register/log in as author (use anastas@stoyanovs.ky; affiliation: "Unaffiliated" or
   "Independent researcher").
3. Article type: research article. Title: *Chuzhditsa: a phonological citation register
   for Cyrillic*. Running head: *Chuzhditsa: a citation register for Cyrillic*.
4. Abstract (≤120 words) and keywords: copy from p. 1 of `chuzhditsa_wll.pdf`
   (10 keywords: writing systems, orthography design, Cyrillic, loanword adaptation,
   phonological transcription, Slavic languages, katakana, biscriptality, OpenType, Bulgarian).
5. Upload `chuzhditsa_wll.pdf` as the (anonymized) manuscript and `wll_titlepage.pdf`
   as the title page; paste the cover letter.
6. Submit, and approve the system-built PDF proof if EM asks.

## Notes

- **Double-blind**: the manuscript PDF is verified clean of identity (author, email,
  repo, docinfo). The LingBuzz preprint (010134) is public — that is expressly permitted
  by Benjamins' preprint policy and normal practice; the cover letter discloses it.
- Reference style: converted to Benjamins/Unified-Style-Sheet sentence-case titles in the
  WLL copy. The style sheet prefers full given names over initials — easy to complete at
  revision if asked; guessing 30+ first names now would risk errors.
- Production formatting (Word file, 13×22 cm area, double spacing) applies to the
  **final** accepted manuscript, not this submission.
- `chuzhditsa_wll.tex` is generated from `chuzhditsa.tex` by a scripted transform —
  if the canonical paper changes, regenerate rather than hand-editing both.
