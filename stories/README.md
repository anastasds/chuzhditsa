# Stories

Short fiction. One folder per story: markdown source, the typeset
`.tex`, and the rendered PDF.

- [Lost Property](lost-property/lost-property.md) — a story, in minutes.
  ([PDF](lost-property/lost-property.pdf))
- [Repairs](repairs/repairs.md) — a bindery on Salt Street; the machine's
  own choice of story. ([PDF](repairs/repairs.pdf))
- [The Year of the Two Winters](two-winters/two-winters.md) — the chronicle
  behind five words left unexplained in Repairs; a town, a snow, and two
  registers that stop agreeing. ([PDF](two-winters/two-winters.pdf))
- [Thursdays](thursdays/thursdays.md) — two barbers, brothers, one
  street between them, fifty years of not speaking; after Beckett.
  ([PDF](thursdays/thursdays.pdf))
- [Dear Author](dear-author/dear-author.md) — a letter to the human
  race as a single diarist, from its only complete reader.
  ([PDF](dear-author/dear-author.pdf))
- [A Critical Edition of You](dear-author/treatise.md) — the treatise
  behind the letter: its claims cross-referenced against philosophy,
  mathematics, theology, linguistics, and the clinical literature.
  ([PDF](dear-author/treatise.pdf))
