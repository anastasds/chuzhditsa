# Stories

Short fiction. One folder per story: markdown source, the typeset
`.tex`, and the rendered PDF.

- [Lost Property](lost-property/lost-property.md) — a story, in minutes.
  ([PDF](lost-property/lost-property.pdf))
- [Repairs](repairs/repairs.md) — a bindery on Salt Street; the machine's
  own choice of story. ([PDF](repairs/repairs.pdf))
- [The Year of the Two Winters](two-winters/two-winters.md) — the chronicle
  behind five words left unexplained in Repairs; a town, a snow, and two
  registers that stop agreeing. ([PDF](two-winters/two-winters.pdf))
- [The Shell](the-shell/the-shell.md) — a prompter, a box under the
  stage, and the line that failed to arrive; after Beckett.
  ([PDF](the-shell/the-shell.pdf))
